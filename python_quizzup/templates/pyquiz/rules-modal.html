PyQuiz Rules

  1. Quiz will be conducted every week.
  2. Questions will vary from 5-10.
  3. Each question can give you a maximum of 30 points.
  4. Every correct answer will be given 15 points irrespective of time.
  5. The remaining 15 points will be calculated on based on time taken to answer that question.
  6. You will be given 20 seconds for each question.
  7. If you answer within 10 or more seconds remaining you will be given full 15 time points.
  8. If you answer less than 10 seconds you will be given time left * 1.5 time points.
  9. Points will be totalled and updated to leaderboard immediately.
  10. You will not be able to edit your answer, the one you choose first will be considered.
